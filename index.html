SAMPLER TYPEWRITER
Upload your own image files using button at lower left (may need to scroll).
Switch between source images using number keys or '=' key to advance, '-' key to go back.
Click and drag to select a portion of the source image to sample it. Samples are automatically sequenced on the right.
ENTER to start a new line.
SHIFT plus letter keys a-z, or spacebar, to map the last sample to a key. Typing that key will repeat the sample.
Arrow keys will flip the most recent sample horizontally or vertically.
'/' to rotate the most recent sample in increments of 90 degrees.
At the start of a new line, drag the rectangular handle at the bottom of the cursor to change line height.
SHIFT plus '~' to clear page(will retain saved samples).
At the start of a clear page, slider is available at lower left to set tile spacing.
Multiple KEY BANKS store keyboard mappings. Change key bank using SHIFT and left/right curly {}brackets.
You can also sample from the right side of the canvas (whitespace, or your own composition).
'<' zoom in, '>' zoom out, SHIFT plus arrows to pan.
